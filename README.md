## Aberration, now on [Strata](https://stratasource.org/)!

> [!NOTE]
> Aberration started out as a Portal 2 mod, before development switched to Portal 2: Community Edition. The original repo is [here](https://github.com/DIYLabsED/aberration).

<br>

## How to play
Aberration is now on the Portal 2: Community Edition workshop! Subscribe to it [here](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3712579002).

<br>

Alternatively, you can clone this repository into your P2:CE install's addons folder. 

The addons folder is at `<Your Steam library>/steamapps/common/Portal 2 Community Edition/p2ce/addons`. The P2:CE SDK offers a shortcut to get to this folder.

Run `git clone https://github.com/DIYLabsED/aberration-p2ce.git` in this directory.

<br>

## Third-party assets used
Aberration uses assets from the Portal Mapping and Modding community. These assets are not covered by the CC BY 4.0 license. Most asset links link to posts in the `#custom-assets` channel in the PMaM Discord server. You may need to [join the server](https://discord.com/invite/pmam) and [head to the channel](https://discord.com/channels/830239808596606976/1352655119006699570) to view them.
